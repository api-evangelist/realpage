Property management teams are being asked to make faster leasing decisions while managing higher application volume and increased fraud risk. Applicant screening reports have become a critical control point in that process, directly impacting bad debt exposure, eviction rates, and overall portfolio performance. A well-structured screening approach helps operators standardize decisions, reduce subjectivity, and protect net operating income (NOI).
