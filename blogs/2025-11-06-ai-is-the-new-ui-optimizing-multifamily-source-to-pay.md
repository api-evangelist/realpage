In the dynamic and competitive landscape of multifamily property management, optimizing every facet of operations is crucial. Among these critical areas, the source-to-pay process, which encompasses everything from identifying and selecting vendors to processing invoices and making payments, plays a pivotal role in financial health and operational efficiency. Today’s multifamily market demands more than just traditional methods; it requires a strategic focus on cost control, streamlined workflows, and robust vendor relationships to achieve sustainable success.
