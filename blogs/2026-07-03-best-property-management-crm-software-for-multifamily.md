What is a Property Management CRM System? A property management CRM system is software that helps operators manage prospect and resident relationships across the leasing lifecycle. CRM stands for customer relationship management, which means the system organizes communication, follow-up tasks, lead activity, tour scheduling, and leasing progress in one place.
