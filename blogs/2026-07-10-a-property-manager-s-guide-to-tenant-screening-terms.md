Property management teams rely on tenant screening services to make consistent leasing decisions, manage risk and support compliance requirements. Screening reports often combine information from multiple sources, creating a process that involves both operational and regulatory considerations. For multifamily operators, leasing teams and compliance professionals, understanding common tenant screening terminology helps ensure everyone is working from the same definitions when evaluating applicants and interpreting screening results.
