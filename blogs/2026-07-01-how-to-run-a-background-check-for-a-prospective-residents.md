Tenant screening sits at the intersection of risk management and leasing velocity. For multifamily property management companies, inconsistent or manual screening processes can slow approvals, increase exposure to fraud, and create compliance risk across a portfolio. Property operators are increasingly standardizing and automating background checks to improve decision consistency while maintaining fair housing compliance.
