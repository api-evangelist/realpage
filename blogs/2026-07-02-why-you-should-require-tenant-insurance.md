Property management companies are responsible for balancing resident experience, operational efficiency, and risk management across their portfolios. When an unexpected event occurs, such as a kitchen fire, water leak, or guest injury, questions often arise about who is financially responsible for damages and related costs. While property liability insurance protects the owner and building from broader risks, it does not cover a resident’s personal liability or belongings.
